OneHome.com

Reimagining the real estate discovery experience to build trust, personalization, and revenue for CoreLogic and partner lenders.

Role
Product Manager
Timeframe
2019 – 2021
Company
CoreLogic · Consumer Digital
Team
Product, UX, MLS operations, data science, engineering, GTM

Impact

  • Activation lifted from 6% → 35%
  • 52% monthly re-engagement
  • 22M+ users onboarded
  • $7M mortgage revenue generated

Problem

Legacy MLS portals were clunky, offered little personalization, and struggled with compliance. Users bounced after the first session, and lenders lacked a way to nurture leads, causing stalled mortgage revenue.

Insights

  • • Buyers wanted curated listings and human reassurance before committing to agents or lenders.
  • • MLS partners emphasized strict data governance; any UX innovation had to respect compliance rules.
  • • Loan officers needed signals on buyer readiness to time their outreach.

Bets

We committed to three bets: trust-building UX (transparency, collaboration tools), a Fit Score algorithm blending buyer preferences with inventory data, and lender-integrated journeys for monetization.

Execution

• Conducted 70+ qualitative interviews across buyers, agents, and lenders to map the end-to-end journey.

• Built an MVP in six months, launching to three MLS partners with real-time Fit Score recommendations.

• Introduced collaborative features (shared favorites, guided tours) with compliance checks embedded.

• Enabled lender workflows with in-product messaging and CRM integrations to improve conversion.

Results

Customer outcomes

  • • Activation soared from 6% to 35% within six months.
  • • 52% of users re-engaged monthly thanks to trust-based UX.
  • • Fit Score recommendations increased agent lead acceptance by 28%.

Business impact

  • • Scaled to 22M+ users across partner MLS networks.
  • • Drove $7M in mortgage origination revenue.
  • • Reduced churn among MLS partners with a modern, co-branded experience.

Learnings

Trust is the foundation for monetization. By combining transparent data practices with empathetic UX, we earned permission to introduce lender experiences without eroding user confidence.

What’s next

The team is expanding personalization with affordability insights and AI-driven content. I left a roadmap prioritizing localization, equity-focused recommendations, and additional lender partnerships.
