ClaimsConnect

Rapid MVP to align adjusters, carriers, and policyholders on a single claims collaboration platform.

Role
Product Strategist
Timeframe
2018 · 12-week MVP
Team
Product, design, engineering, adjuster SMEs
Pilots
3 regional carriers

Impact

  • MVP shipped in 12 weeks
  • 40% faster claim triage
  • 93% adjuster satisfaction
  • Unified audit trail for compliance

Problem

Carriers and third-party adjusters relied on spreadsheets, email, and phone calls to coordinate claims. Lack of transparency meant duplicated efforts, slow payouts, and compliance risk.

Approach

• Facilitated design sprints with adjusters and policyholders to map critical pain points.

• Prioritized workflow automation: intake, triage assignments, document capture, and vendor coordination.

• Delivered a modular MVP with audit-ready activity feeds and automated notifications.

Results

Operational outcomes

  • • 40% faster triage decisions through shared queues.
  • • Single audit trail reduced compliance exceptions by 35%.

Adoption & sentiment

  • • 93% CSAT from adjuster beta group.
  • • 2 of 3 pilots expanded to paid contracts within 90 days.

Learnings

Starting small with modular workflows made adoption easier for risk-averse carriers. Co-creating enablement materials with compliance leaders ensured buy-in beyond the pilot cohort.

Next steps

The backlog prioritized integrations with core claims systems, predictive severity scoring, and expanding vendor marketplaces. Handoffs included a roadmap and adoption playbook for scaling nationally.
