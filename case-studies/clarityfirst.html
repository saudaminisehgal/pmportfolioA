ClarityFirst Closing Platform

Digitizing commercial real estate closings across 40+ states for First American, delivering automation, transparency, and 99% faster transaction times.

Role
Senior Product Manager & Product Lead
Timeframe
2022 – 2024
Company
First American Title · Commercial Services
Team
12 cross-functional squads (product, design, engineering, operations)

Impact

  • $3B+ transaction volume processed
  • $200M+ incremental revenue
  • 99% reduction in transaction time
  • 4.3 / 5 internal satisfaction

Problem

Commercial closing teams relied on legacy systems and email threads to shepherd multi-million-dollar deals. Each closing required an average of 140 manual steps, creating error-prone handoffs, delayed funding, and low satisfaction among escrow officers and customers.

Insights

  • • Escrow officers spent 40% of their day double-entering data across systems.
  • • Customers demanded status transparency but were hesitant to adopt new tools without trust signals.
  • • Compliance reviews were bottlenecks due to inconsistent documentation and audit trails.

Bets

I aligned executives around three portfolio bets: build a single source of truth, automate repetitive tasks, and surface predictive insights for risk mitigation. Each bet had defined success metrics tied to cycle time and revenue recognition.

Execution

• Stood up dual-track agile ceremonies with 12 squads, unifying product, design, engineering, and operations into one roadmap.

• Partnered with underwriting and compliance to codify requirements into reusable automation rules.

• Launched phased MVPs starting with high-volume markets, incorporating AI-assisted document parsing and guided workflows.

• Built executive dashboards showing time-to-close and conversion metrics to maintain stakeholder alignment.

Results

Operational wins

  • • Reduced clicks per closing by 77% and screens by 67%.
  • • Real-time alerts cut post-closing corrections by 54%.
  • • Workflow automation eliminated 1.2M manual tasks annually.

Business impact

  • • Enabled $3B+ in commercial volume through the platform.
  • • Created $200M+ incremental revenue opportunity via premium services.
  • • Lifted NPS for internal users from 3.1 to 4.3 / 5.

Learnings

High-trust enterprise adoption requires relentless change management. Embedding enablement specialists into every squad, along with clear ROI dashboards, ensured operations embraced automation instead of reverting to legacy tools.

What’s next

The roadmap extends to underwriting analytics, deeper lender integrations, and AI-driven anomaly detection. I set up a prioritized backlog and governance rituals so the team can continue iterating with confidence.
